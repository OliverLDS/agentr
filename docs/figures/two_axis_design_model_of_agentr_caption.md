Figure 2. Two-axis design model of agentr.

The diagram separates subsystem design from workflow design while showing their interaction through agent design. The subsystem axis captures `RWM`, `PG`, `AE`, `IAC`, and `LA`; the workflow axis captures nodes, edges, rules, review state, and human-required gates. The figure emphasizes that the two axes should not be collapsed into one representation, even though design decisions propagate across both.
