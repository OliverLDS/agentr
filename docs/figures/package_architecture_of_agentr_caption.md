Figure 3. Package architecture of agentr.

The diagram shows the major public and supporting objects in `agentr`, including cognitive and affective state containers, the `AgentCore`, the `Scaffolder`, design artifacts such as `AgentSpec` and `SubsystemSpec`, workflow proposal objects, and the runtime-oriented `IntelligentAgent`. It also shows the constrained LLM bridge into `Scaffolder`, persistent design artifacts, and the explicit exclusion of execution-layer concerns from the package boundary.
