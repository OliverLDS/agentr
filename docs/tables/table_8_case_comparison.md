# Table 8. Comparison Of The Two Illustrative Design Cases

Caption: Comparison of the two illustrative design cases.

| Case | Task type | Likely subsystem mix | Main tacit knowledge | Key human gates | Main refinement focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Economic analysis / visualization | Analytical and explanatory workflow over data and charts | `PG` strong, `AE` strong, `RWM` optional or light, `IAC` limited, `LA` minimal | Indicator choice, chart selection, comparison logic, interpretability rules | Review of assumptions, review of visual communication, publication approval | Surface analytic and visualization rules that are often left implicit in coarse workflows |
| Market investment suggestion writing | Interpretive and communication-sensitive workflow for recommendation text | `PG` moderate, `AE` moderate, `RWM` stronger, `IAC` stronger, `LA` possible | Risk framing, compliance caution, uncertainty wording, recommendation boundaries | Review of claims and uncertainty, review of compliance-sensitive wording, final human approval | Surface communicative and compliance constraints that govern acceptable suggestion writing |
